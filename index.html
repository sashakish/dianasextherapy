About Diana

Sex & Relationship Therapy

I view sexuality from the perspective of pleasure and quality of life, not from a dysfunction-based model. My professional and personal goal is to assist my clients in the process of self-healing and growth, helping them realize their full potential through expressing their sexuality. My work is incredibly rewarding, and I truly love being able to empower my clients.

Whether you want to increase your confidence, explore fantasies, or discover a new level of intimacy with your partner, I can help. My specialties include treating issues with erectile dysfunction, sexual trauma, loss of desire, relationships in couples, women, and men.

I have a PhD in Human Sexuality from the Institute for Advanced Study of Human Sexuality, as well as a graduate degree (MSW) in Clinical Social Work. Additionally, I am a California State Licensed Clinical Social Worker (LCSW #26883) and Certified Clinical Sexologist.
